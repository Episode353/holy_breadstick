Go-------------------------------------------------------------------
    Use "Go" followed by a direction to go that direction instantly
    For example "Go North" or "Go East"

Goto------------------------------------------------------------------
    Travels to specific coordinates, but will take an ammount of time
    For example "Go to 12,45"

Arrow Keys Movement---------------------------------------------------
    Use the Arrow Keys to navigate quickly, without typing

Look-------------------------------------------------------------------
    Look around and see what is in the area

Reload-----------------------------------------------------------------
    Reload the Program
    
Quit-------------------------------------------------------------------
    Quit the program
